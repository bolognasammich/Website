Greetings Friend! My name is Sam. Welcome to my website!

I received my Bachelor's in the summer of 2021 from Illinois State University’s Creative Technologies program with a focus on video editing and production.

I am passionate about the creative process! Working at Nexstar as a digital producer has allowed me to strengthen my skills by interviewing national politicians, shooting live events, and writing stories for CIproud.com and WMBD/WYZZ social media.

When I’m not working you can usually find me watching a movie or listening to vinyl. Please take a look at my portfolio and reach out with any questions or inquiries!
